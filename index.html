Tony Gillies

Tony Gillies Intuitive Artist
Born 1967 in Alexandra New Zealand and father to three awesome sons. After 20 years in the signwriting industry I decided painting was a passion I would like to make a career.

In 2004 I decided to do 50 practice paintings. Painting from my photographs of waterfalls, trees, posts, sunrises, roads and korus.

The attraction to korus was undeniable. In 2011 after a 5 year break from painting I focused on creating my koru series incorporating messages of power and love. Utilising my signwriting abilities within life's precious words.
