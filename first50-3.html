First Fifty


This is a collection of my paintings when I started to became serious about painting. Gifted the majority to friends and loved ones.

NOTE: These painting are not up for sale and are one off pieces.

Orange Koru, No. 38

Another soft Koru gifted to Emma Herriott & Dean Williamson. Love you guys.


Sunflower, No. 40

A small painting of a sunflower growing at my Waitati paradise. Still hangs in my home.


Red Hot Pokers, No. 41

Had a couple of goes at painting these. Gifted to my sister Kelly Sullivan. Love you xo.


Whites Bay, No. 42

Breathtaking sunrise enjoyed with Vicki Harrison on new years day. Gifted to my youngest son. Love you Dylan.

Road Narrows Milford Sound, No. 46

Mitre Peak, No. 47

Fencepost with hinge. No. 48

Another awesome fencepost with loads of character. Hangs in my home alongside No. 45.


Milford Road, No. 49

This painting of the Milford Road came out looking quite realistic and mum and dad loved it so it is theirs. Love you Mum and Dad.


Little Blues, No. 50

Finished off my first 50 practice paintings with these cute little flowers. Hangs in my kitchen.
